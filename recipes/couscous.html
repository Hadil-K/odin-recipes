Tunisian Couscous

Description

Tunisian couscous is a flavorful and hearty dish, often prepared on Fridays or special occasions. It’s made with steamed semolina, vegetables, and either lamb, chicken, or fish, all simmered in a rich, spiced tomato-based sauce.

Ingredients

  • Couscous grains
  • Olive oil
  • Onion
  • Garlic
  • Tomato paste
  • Harissa
  • Spices (cumin, caraway, paprika, turmeric)
  • Chickpeas
  • Carrots
  • Zucchini
  • Potatoes
  • Meat (usually lamb, chicken, or fish)

Steps

  1. In a large pot, heat olive oil and sauté chopped onion and garlic.
  2. Add tomato paste and a spoon of harissa, stir for a minute.
  3. Add spices, chickpeas, meat, and enough water to cover. Simmer until meat is tender.
  4. Add vegetables and cook until soft.
  5. Steam couscous over the sauce using a couscoussier (or steam in a bowl, fluffing with olive oil).
  6. Serve couscous in a large dish, pour the sauce with vegetables and meat on top.
