Ingredients

  • garlic
  • tomatoes
  • oil

Steps

  1. Boil the spaghetti in salted water according to package instructions. Drain and set aside.
  2. In a pan, heat olive oil and sauté garlic until fragrant (about 1 minute).
  3. Add the crushed tomatoes, oregano, salt, and pepper. Simmer for 10–15 minutes.
  4. Toss in the cooked spaghetti and mix until evenly coated with sauce.
  5. Serve hot, topped with grated Parmesan and fresh basil if desired.
